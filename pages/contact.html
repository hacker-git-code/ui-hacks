Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about BuilderSpace

What is BuilderSpace?

BuilderSpace is a social media platform designed specifically for startup founders, developers, and creators. It provides tools and features tailored to the needs of builders, including project showcasing, networking opportunities, and knowledge sharing.

Is BuilderSpace free to use?

Yes, BuilderSpace offers a free tier with access to core features. We also offer premium plans with additional features for power users and teams.

How do I join BuilderSpace?

Joining is easy! Simply click the "Sign Up" button at the top of the page and follow the registration process. You can create an account using your email or sign up with your Google or GitHub account.

Can I showcase my projects on BuilderSpace?

Absolutely! BuilderSpace is designed to help you showcase your work. You can create detailed project pages, share updates, and receive feedback from the community.

How do Live Rooms work?

Live Rooms are audio spaces where you can host discussions, AMAs, or community events. Any user can create a room and invite others to join as speakers or listeners.
